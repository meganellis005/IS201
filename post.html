Hobbies

What I do in my free time

Photography and Travel

Photography and travel have both interested me since I can remember. I enjoy stopping time for a moment, being able to reflect, look back on the past and at places where I didn't have enough time to fully grasp. These two passions have been my favorite and most absorbing hobbies in my life.

Photographs taken by Megan Ellis in Europe and the Middle East. To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.

Watercolor

This hobby is what I do when I need to unwind. I haven't mastered this form of art, but I enjoy creating. My favorite subjects are flowers. I enjoy creating art as much as I enjoy looking at it.

Watercolor paintings by Megan Ellis. “Moonlight is sculpture; sunlight is painting.” -Nathaniel Hawthorne

Extreme Sports

Spending time outside gives me energy. Connecting with nature is an important part of my life, whether it takes the form of going to the park or whitewater rafting.

Moments in the great outdoors. “Look deep into nature and you will understand everything better.” – Albert Einstein For More quotes, see brainyquotes.com
